HTML Enities

HTML Enities are preceded by & and end with ;. Used to display reserved characters (which would otherwise be interpreted as HTML code), and invisible characters (like non-breaking spaces)

  •   & + nbsp; is for indenting
  •     the more it is used.
  •       the more that text is indented.
  • < & + lt; prints the less than symbol
  • > & + gt; prints the greater than symbol
  • © & + copy; is for the copyright symbol
  • MDN abbr lets us undlerline abbreviations, but we need to add a title atribute for it to be effective and show the meaning for the abreviation on hover.Doesn't work with screen readers, or assistive technologies.
